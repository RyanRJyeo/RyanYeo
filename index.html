Ripple

It is said that almost anything can be aesthetically artistic if it is absolutely symmetrical.

Enter Ripple, an art creator that allow users to create various symmetrical arts with a few clicks of a button. Be it a novice artist or Picasso himself, Ripple allows the artwork created to be perfectly duplicated on four sides, achieving symmetrical aesthetics.

As a final touch, users can download the artwork in the form of a PNG file for personal enjoyment, or to use the artwork in other personal projects or customizable goods.

FileUpp

Many companies are moving towards a paperless movement due to many practical reasons. Paper files can be easily damaged or lost especially during transitions.

Therefore, it can be challenging to manage various documents regarding one's clients, students, or patients. Enter FileUpp, a customer relationship management app that not only allow users to create their case files easily, but they can also seamlessly share those case files with their teammates or their superiors.

Users can now have a centralized source of information regarding their cases and work with their teammates to update those case files simultaneously.

Jobberwocky

We all know that the job search process can be long sometimes and it is hard to determine the right cultural fit.

Enter Jobberwocky, the job-search portal that is easy to use, more casual, and less intimidating. With a clear user interface, both users and companies can update their profiles or job postings quickly and easily. Thereafter, users and job postings are displayed to one another based on our complex search algorithm.

The app also includes a chat feature to facilitate fast and convenient communication between users and companies once they have matched each other.
